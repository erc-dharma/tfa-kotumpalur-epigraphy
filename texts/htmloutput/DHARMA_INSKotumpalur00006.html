Koṭumpāḷūr, Aivarkōyil, inscription of the time of kesarivarman, year unknown

Metadata

Identifier:

INSKotumpalur00006

Summary:

Lacunose inscription. Donation of gold for the Mahādeva of the Aintaḷi at Koṭumpāḷūr, probably for the upkeep of a perpetual lamp.
