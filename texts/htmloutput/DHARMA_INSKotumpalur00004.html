Translation by Emmanuel Francis

1Prosperity! Fortune!

1Fifteenth year of the king Rājakesarivarman.

1[... in the] Uṟattūrkkūṟṟam [...]

2Naṅkaiyār Aṉantam Paḻiyili ... (vaḻiyu)

Commentary

The inscription stops short in line 2 after vaḻiyu and seems to have been left unfinished.

2Naṅkaiyār Aṉantam Paḻiyili (“our unbounded and irreproachable lady”?) is possibly the donor.
